# Photo Capture SOP for Inventory

## Purpose
Ensure consistent, high-quality product photos for the Quick Add workflow so items look reliable online and in-store.

## Equipment
- Android phone with the POS web app (camera enabled)
- Neutral background (white foam board or plain wall)
- Ring light or LED panel with diffuser
- Small turntable or marked floor mat for angle consistency

## Environment setup
1. **Lighting:** Place the light at 45° above and in front of the shoe. Avoid mixing warm and cool light.
2. **Background:** Use the same background daily. Wipe dust/creases.
3. **Staging:** Keep a lint roller and microfiber cloth nearby to clean shoes quickly.
4. **Phone settings:** Enable gridlines, disable flash (to prevent harsh shadows), set resolution to high.

## Shot list per product
1. **Hero angle (45° front):** Main image used in listings. Frame shoe diagonally, include full outsole.
2. **Side profile (medial):** Straight-on left or right side showing logo/stripes.
3. **Top view:** Camera directly overhead to show laces/insole.
4. **Heel/back view:** Highlights heel counter and branding.
5. **Sole tread:** Tilt shoe forward to capture outsole pattern.
6. **Detail close-up:** Stitching, material, or unique feature. Optional for budget lines.

> Minimum requirement: shots 1–3 for older stock; add 4–6 for new arrivals or marketing campaigns.

## Shooting steps
1. Open **Quick Add → Take Photo**.
2. Place first shoe on turntable markers.
3. Tap focus on the logo, wait for exposure to balance.
4. Capture hero angle; review for blur. Retake if needed.
5. Rotate shoe for side, top, heel, sole, and detail shots, capturing each before moving on.
6. After the last shot, proceed to the details form without exiting the camera flow.

## Quality checklist (before saving)
- ✔ Lighting even, no harsh shadows.
- ✔ Background clean, no props visible.
- ✔ Shoe fills ~80% of frame.
- ✔ Images sharp—zoom 100% to verify.
- ✔ Correct pair/size tagged in the variant wizard.

## File management
- Photos auto-upload via the POS. If offline, the app queues images. Reconnect Wi-Fi before the end of shift.
- For manual backups, sync the device with Google Drive weekly.

## Troubleshooting
- **Glare/hotspots:** Diffuse light with tracing paper or bounce off white wall.
- **Shadows too strong:** Move shoe 30 cm from backdrop and raise the light.
- **Color cast:** Use daylight bulbs (5000–5500K) and turn off ambient yellow lights.

## Review cadence
- Team lead reviews sample photos every Friday.
- Maintain a shared album with best-in-class shots for reference.

# Quick Add Laminated Cheat Sheet (Print on A5)

**Front side – Steps**
1. Tap **Tambah Barang Cepat**.
2. **Foto**: Take 3 angles (depan 45°, samping, atas). Retake if blur.
3. **Brand**: Pilih atau tambah baru.
4. **Nama Model**: Ketik nama singkat + warna.
5. **Kategori**: Pilih dari daftar (Sneakers, Sandal, Formal, Kids, dst.).
6. **Harga Jual**: Input angka saja (contoh `450000`).
7. **Catatan**: Isi kondisi khusus (contoh `Display`, `Sale 20%`).
8. **Varian & Stok**: Pilih skala ukuran → centang ukuran yang ada → isi stok per ukuran.
9. **Simpan**: Pastikan muncul notifikasi hijau "Barang tersimpan".

**Back side – Tips & Reminders**
- Gunakan Wi-Fi toko agar foto cepat terunggah.
- Jika brand belum ada, ketik nama penuh lalu simpan.
- Untuk stok lama, tulis catatan `Legacy` agar mudah dilacak.
- Cek ulang total stok sebelum simpan.
- Bila ada error merah, baca pesannya lalu perbaiki (biasanya SKU kembar atau harga kosong).
- Hubungi Admin lewat WhatsApp jika butuh reset password atau import massal.

Lamination note: Print color, cut to A5, laminate, and place near photo station and cashier desk.
